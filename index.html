Designers Against AI

Designers Against AI is a collaborative platform that compiles designers who reject the use of artificial intelligence in their work.

Over the past decade, we have witnessed how tech giants have fueled the unrestrained development of algorithmic computational models that attempt to emulate human intelligence. Machine learning algorithms require vast amounts of data to function properly. It has been proven that during the learning process, copyrighted material is used without proper authorization.

By joining the list, designers guarantee their work does not include content generated by Copilot, Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, Grok, LLaMa, Deepseek, Veo 3, Sora, Midjourney, Dall-e, or Stable Diffusion.

Submissions are reviewed and added weekly. Incomplete or unverifiable submissions are not published. Contributors are encouraged to prioritize honesty.
